03 — Background

About

I've spent 8 years at GLG — a PE-backed global expert network — managing a $14M+ annual book of business across the organizations that shape how enterprises make decisions: Deloitte, Accenture, Gartner, KPMG, AlixPartners, Stax, Kearney, and Roland Berger.

Most of what I've built didn't exist before I built it. Health scoring systems, renewal pipeline architecture, Salesforce-based portfolio dashboards, team playbooks, CoE design. I operate at the intersection of client-facing delivery and operational infrastructure — the place where data becomes decisions.

I'm now deepening the analytical side of that work. The part of the job I've always found most energizing isn't the account management — it's the discovery phase: sitting with messy data, finding patterns before you know what you're looking for, and surfacing the signal that changes what the business does next.

My Google Data Analytics capstone — behavioral dropout prediction on longitudinal wearable data — was the first time I got to do that work from scratch. It was genuinely fun. I'm building toward more of it.
